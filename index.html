    About Me

In this role, I was responsible for ensuring the highest standards of quality through meticulous testing and validation. I utilized DevOps practices, C# for scripting and unit testing, Selenium for automated testing, and conducted thorough testing of REST APIs to ensure optimal performance and reliability.

In this role, I contributed to a team effort to rebuild the Apiary nvestment Fund website. The redesigned platform enabled users to view their trades, balances, forex variations, market expectations, goals achievement, and available courses for learning how to trade, significantly enhancing both functionality and user experience.


    Beyond this, I have worked on over 20 front-end projects, further honing my skills in creating dynamic, user-friendly web interfaces and delivering high-quality solutions across various applications.

I am eager to tackle new challenges and leverage my diverse background to drive innovation and deliver impactful results.
